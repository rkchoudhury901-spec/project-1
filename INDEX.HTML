Our Story

Dahibara Aludum is more than just food - it's a tradition, a memory, and a celebration of Odia culture. Since 1985, we've been perfecting this iconic street food recipe that has captured hearts across Odisha.

Our secret? Fresh ingredients, traditional recipes, and the love we put into every single serving. From the crispy outer layer of dahibara to the flavorful potato curry and chilled yogurt - every element is crafted to perfection.
